## An intelligent eye-care assistant that understands user behavior instead of simply counting time.
## Introduction

Considering the lack of suitable eye-care reminder applications for users who spend long hours working on computers, we designed this project to provide a smarter and more user-friendly solution.

This project monitors your computer usage time and intelligently determines when you need to take a break. Don't worry about interruptions during inactive periods — if you haven't used your keyboard or mouse for a while, and you are not watching videos or reading documents, the system will automatically detect your inactive state and temporarily pause the timer.

The break intervals are fully customizable, allowing users to adjust them according to their own habits and needs. By default, the recommended settings are based on national health guidelines for reducing visual fatigue caused by prolonged computer use.

To make break time more enjoyable and engaging, we have created a series of cute Nailong (a popular cartoon character) animations. These animations guide users through a set of eye exercises and encourage healthy break activities, such as taking a short walk, having a quick nap, enjoying a cup of coffee, or simply relaxing away from the screen.

Our goal is to make eye protection a natural and pleasant part of everyday computer usage rather than an annoying reminder.
